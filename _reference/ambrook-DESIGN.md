# Ambrook — Anchor Reference (Refero)

> **Role for Bighorn:** warmth + authenticity. Keeps the site from feeling cold/clinical;
> shows how candid real work-scene photography + an earthy palette + a gold accent reads
> as "trustworthy, field-tested, grounded."

**Source:** https://ambrook.com · Refero style `b11e1e78-3c62-45df-bf28-17c97718ed7d`
**Theme:** light · **Mood:** "rustic ledger on cream parchment, field-tested identity"

## Why it's an anchor
Ambrook serves agriculture — a blue-collar, hands-on audience adjacent to Bighorn's
trades. It proves a B2B site can feel warm and human without being soft: candid
photography of real people in work environments, an earthy neutral palette, a single
warm gold CTA, and authoritative display type. Bighorn already owns a gold — Ambrook
validates it as the accent.

## Key tokens
- **Canvas:** Greige Canvas `#fcfaf1` warm off-white · Warm Paper `#efe9e0` secondary
- **Ink:** Soil `#211b15` body · Moss `#252a23` headings + dark hero/footer sections
- **Accent:** Harvest Gold `#e8b672` for primary CTAs (cf. Bighorn's existing `#C19B3D`)
- **Secondary accent:** Forest Fern `#7a9779` for outline buttons / strokes
- **Type:** authoritative display (gravitas, wide tracking at large sizes) + utilitarian
  sans body, line-height ≥ 1.5 for comfort
- **Radius:** cards `7.5px`, buttons/inputs `3.75px`, large cards `11.25px`
- **Shadows:** none — depth via earthy borders + background shifts
- **Layout:** 1200px max, 45px section gap, alternating canvas/paper/moss section rhythm

## Do
- Warm off-white canvas, not stark white — feels grounded, not clinical
- Candid photography of real people in real work environments (slight desaturation OK)
- Single warm gold CTA; reserve it strictly for primary actions
- Alternate light canvas / warm paper / dark moss sections for vertical rhythm
- Authoritative display type with generous tracking

## Don't
- Don't use generic cold grays (`#eee`, `#ccc`) — pick warm earthy neutrals
- Don't use strong drop shadows or glossy effects
- Don't use tight body line-heights — keep copy comfortable (≥1.5)
